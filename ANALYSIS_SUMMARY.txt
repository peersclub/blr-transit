KEY STATISTICS & INSIGHTS
================================================================================

THE PROBLEM:
  - Rank: 3rd worst traffic globally (TomTom 2024)
  - Speed: 17.6 km/h average (essentially crawling)
  - Time Lost: 132 hours per driver annually
  - Economic Impact: ‚Çπ20,000 crores annually
  - Growth Gap: 12% vehicles vs 7% roads = 5% deficit
  - Daily Waste: 2.8 million liters of fuel

THE SOLUTION:
  - Fleet Scaling: 100 ‚Üí 500 ‚Üí 2,000 buses (3 phases)
  - Target Market: 240,000 commuters (30% of 800K IT workers)
  - CO‚ÇÇ Reduction: 37% (15,000 ‚Üí 9,500 tons/day)
  - Time Savings: 45 minutes per trip, 20 hours/month
  - Revenue: ‚Çπ700 crores monthly
  - Profit Margin: 28.5%
  - Pricing: ‚Çπ3,500/month (AC) or ‚Çπ1,750 (regular) = 60% cheaper

  2. Electronic City: 100K employees
